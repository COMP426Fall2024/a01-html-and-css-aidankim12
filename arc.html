More on One Piece

One Piece, having been serialized for over 20 years, is really long. Currently the manga has over 1120 chapters! The series is divided into sagas and arcs. Colloquially, the sagas are referred to as arcs. I'm willing to bet no one really talks about individual arcs such as 'Romance Dawn' or 'Jaya'. Instead, they will just refer to the name of the saga 'East Blue' or 'Skypiea'. Below you can select an arc to learn more about it (and pressing the button will totally do something).
