Origins

One Piece is a manga written Eiichiro Oda. It began publication in the Weekly Shounen Jump in 1997 and was adapted into an anime by Toei Animations just a few years after! Oda is very particular about public appearances. Most pictures you'll find of him are extremely outdated. Pictured to the right is the caricature he uses for weekly comments from authors.

Synopsis

Gold Roger's words (as seen on the home page) sent the people of the One Piece world into a frenzy in search of his treasure which was hidden in the Grand Line. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy who dreams of becoming the king of the pirates. With the power of the Gum-Gum fruit, he sets sail for the Grand Line at the age of 17 and forms his crew, the Strawhat Pirates. There he makes many new friends and encounters dangerous enemies.

Why should you watch it?
